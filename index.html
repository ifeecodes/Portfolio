About Me

I am Ifechukwu Ikenta, a web developer and UI/UX designer focused on creating clean, modern, and responsive websites that help brands stand out online. I combine frontend craftsmanship with backend development to build full-featured, user-friendly websites that engage visitors and support business growth.

By working with me, you gain:

  • A modern and responsive website that works seamlessly across devices
  • Clean, well-structured code for easy maintenance and scalability
  • Robust backend functionality powered by PHP and MySQL
  • Thoughtful UI/UX design that improves user experience and engagement
  • A visually polished website that strengthens your brand identity

Whether you need a business website, landing page, or a database-driven web application, my goal is to transform your ideas into a professional digital experience that attracts visitors and converts them into clients
